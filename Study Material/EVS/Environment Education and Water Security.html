B] Our Core Objectives

Our mission is structured around clear, actionable goals to create tangible change.

Raise Awareness & Empower

Educate on water as a finite resource and equip communities with skills for localized solutions like rainwater harvesting.

Influence Policy & Science

Foster understanding of water policies and motivate engagement with innovative technologies for conservation.

"Water fuels every aspect of life. Without water security there can be no national security."

SWP offers a pragmatic, science-based approach to water security, reflecting local geographic and cultural conditions. We focus on causes, not symptoms.

C] Our Aim: The Pillars of Water Security

Health & Livelihoods

Water is essential for human survival, hygiene, and the activities that sustain life: agriculture, energy, and transportation.

Economies & Ecosystems

Improving water security fosters economic growth and protects the ecosystems we all depend on.

D] Our Methodology: Adaptive & Collaborative

We build capacity through diverse, tailored actions:

  • Developing or rehabilitating water structures and networks.
  • Promoting green infrastructure (reforestation, wetland restoration).
  • Initiating behavior and policy changes.

We bring disparate groups together to reach a consensus, moving beyond uninformed decisions. Our partnership with UNESCO and UNEP has been pivotal in advancing environmental education globally through major international conferences since 1977.

E] Historical Context & Observation

UNESCO's leadership in environmental education dates to 1948 with the creation of the IUCN. The co-organization of four major international conferences has consistently highlighted education's pivotal role in sustainable development.

Education extends beyond the classroom to include aquariums, zoos, parks, media campaigns, and public outreach.

F] Conclusion: The Path Forward

We provide the tools to address current and future water risks. The trend in environmental education is towards informed decision-making based on data and experience, integrated into core curricula.
